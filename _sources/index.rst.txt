Welcome to PyCSP's documentation!
#################################

**PyCSP**  (Python-based Crystal Structure Prediction) is a robust, open-source Python library 
for Crystal Structure Prediction. PyCSP is dedicated to quick and easy operations, so that people 
can get the results they want without taking too much time.

Check out the :doc:`./usage/index` section for further information, including how to :doc:`\ install` 
the project. Of course, you can also click `here <https://pyCSP.readthedocs.io/_/downloads/en/latest/pdf/>`_ 
for a pdf instruction.

.. note::

   The PySCP is under active development.
